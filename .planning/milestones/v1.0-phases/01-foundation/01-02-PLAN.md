    - liquidityScore: z.number() (0-1)
    - collectorArchetype: z.union([z.string(), z.null()])
    - missingSetGoals: z.array(z.string())
    - recommendedActions: z.array(z.string())
    - priceDataAsOf: z.union([z.string(), z.null()])
    - priceConfidence: PriceConfidenceSchema

    - comparable_collectors: z.array(z.string())

    - share_card_badges: z.array(z.string()) — badge labels
    - Export CollectorArchetypeSchema and CollectorArchetype type
